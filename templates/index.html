About Us

Our Mission

At Die Gewinner Debate Academie, we are dedicated to empowering the youth of Togo with the skills of critical thinking, confident public speaking, and effective argumentation. We provide a platform for intellectual growth and civic engagement.

DGDA in Togo

Founded in Lomé, DGDA is committed to building a vibrant debate culture in the region. We work with students from various backgrounds to foster a love for reasoned discussion and informed public discourse.

Notre Mission (French)

À l'Académie de Débat Die Gewinner, nous nous engageons à donner aux jeunes Togolais les compétences de la pensée critique, de la prise de parole en public et de l'argumentation efficace. Nous offrons une plateforme pour la croissance intellectuelle et l'engagement civique.

DGDA au Togo (French)

Fondée à Lomé, la DGDA s'est engagée à bâtir une culture de débat dynamique dans la région. Nous travaillons avec des étudiants de divers horizons pour favoriser l'amour du débat raisonné et du discours public éclairé.

Our Programs

For Beginners

Our introductory workshops focus on the fundamentals of debate, including research techniques, argument structure, and confident delivery. Perfect for those new to the world of competitive debate.

For Experienced Debaters

Advanced coaching and strategy sessions prepare experienced debaters for national and international tournaments. We focus on refining complex arguments and mastering specific formats.

Academic Training

We provide in-depth training on various debate formats, including British Parliamentary (BP) and Public Forum (PF), ensuring our debaters are versatile and competitive.
